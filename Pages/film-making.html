Film Making

Director

The film-making diploma course in Direction equips aspiring filmmakers with the skills to bring their creative vision to life. Students learn the art of storytelling, script analysis, and effective communication with actors and crew members. They develop a keen understanding of shot composition, scene visualization, and film aesthetics, becoming proficient in managing all aspects of film production.Candidates should have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized educational board. Some institutes may require a bachelor's degree in any discipline for admission.
