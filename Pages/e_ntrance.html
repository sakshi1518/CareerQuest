ENTRANCE EXAMINATION

  • JEE Main (Joint Entrance Examination-Main):

    Conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), this is the primary entrance exam for admission to prestigious engineering institutes like the India Institutes of Technology (IITs) and the National Institutes of Technology (NITs) GFTIs (Government Funded Technical Institutes), and various other engineering colleges across the country. JEE Main is typically conducted twice a year, usually in January and April. Students can appear for both attempts, and the best score among the two is considered for admission.

  • JEE Advanced:

    JEE Advanced is another national-level entrance exam conducted in India. It is the second stage of the Joint Entrance Examination and is conducted for admission to the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and some other top engineering institutes in the country. After qualifying the JEE Main, candidates can appear for the JEE Advanced to seek admission to the IITs. JEE Advanced is usually held once a year.

  • BITSAT (Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test):

    BITSAT, which stands for Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test, is an entrance examination conducted by BITS Pilani, India. It is used to select candidates for admission to various integrated undergraduate engineering programs offered by the different campuses of BITS Pilani, namely BITS Pilani, BITS Goa, and BITS Hyderabad.

    Learn More

  • VITEEE (VIT Engineering Entrance Examination):

    VITEEE, which stands for Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination, is an entrance exam conducted by Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) for admission to its various B.Tech programs. VIT is a renowned private university in India with campuses in Vellore, Chennai, Bhopal, and Amaravati.

    Learn More

  • SRMJEEE (SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination):

    It is an entrance exam conducted by SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly known as SRM University) for admission to its various B.Tech programs offered at its campuses in Kattankulathur, Ramapuram, Vadapalani, Ghaziabad (NCR), and Amaravati. SRMJEEE is usually conducted once a year, generally in the months of April and May.

    Learn More

  • Manipal Entrance Test (MET):

    It is an entrance exam conducted by Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), which is a prominent private university in India. MET is conducted for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs offered by Manipal Academy of Higher Education. MET is typically conducted once a year for most of the programs. However, for some courses like B.Tech, it may be conducted multiple times during the admission cycle.
