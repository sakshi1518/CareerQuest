  • Staff Selection Commission Combined Higher Secondary Level (SSC CHSL):

    SSC CHSL is a competitive examination conducted by the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) in India. The exam is held to recruit candidates for various posts in ministries, departments, and organizations of the Government of India.The SSC CHSL examination is typically conducted once a year and is open to candidates who have completed their higher secondary education (10+2) or equivalent from a recognized board.   • State Public Service Commission Examination (PSC):

    State Public Service Commission Examination (PSC) is a competitive examination conducted by State Public Service Commissions in India. Each state in India has its own State Public Service Commission, which is responsible for recruiting candidates for various civil services and government positions within the state. The State PSC conducts these examinations to fill vacancies in various state government departments and services, including administrative services, police services, revenue services, forest services, and more. The exams aim to select competent individuals who can contribute to the effective functioning of the state administration and provide public services.
