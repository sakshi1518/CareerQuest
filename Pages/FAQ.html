FAQs(Frequently Asked Questions)

  • Who can benefit from Career Quest?
    Career Quest is designed for students who have completed their 10th and 12th grades and are seeking guidance for their future career paths.

  • Is the guidance optimized to my interests and strengths?
    Yes, our guidance is optimiized to your interests, strengths, and educational background to help you explore suitable career options.

  • How do I access the Career Quest services?
    Simply visit our website and explore the various resources and tools available. You may also contact us directly for personalized assistance.

  • Are the career assessments and recommendations free?
    Yes, our basic career assessments and recommendations are free to all users.

  • Can I get guidance on specific courses after 10 and 12?
    Yes, we provide information and advice on various courses available after completing 10th and 12th grades.

  • How can I get information regarding various exams and tests?
    Simply, go to our tests and exams sections and yo will get all information.

  • How can I stay updated with the latest career trends and opportunities?
    We regularly update our website with the latest career trends, industry insights, and job opportunities to keep you informed.

  • Is my personal information safe with Career Guidance After 10 and 12?
    Absolutely! We prioritize the security and confidentiality of your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties without your consent.

  • Are the career options suitable for students from different educational boards?
    Yes, our options are thoroughly researched to cater to students from various educational boards and backgrounds.

  • Can I seek guidance on career changes and further studies after graduation?
    While our primary focus is on students after 10th and 12th, we also offer resources for those seeking career changes and further studies after graduation.

  • How can I contact your support team for additional help?
    If you have any questions or need further assistance, you can reach out to our support team through the contact information provided on our website. We are here to help you!
