ELIGIBILITY

  • Educational Qualification:

    Applicants should have completed their higher secondary education (12th grade) or an equivalent qualification from a recognized board or institution. The primary subjects required are Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM). Some institutions may also consider Computer Science or Biology as an alternative to Chemistry or as an additional subject.

  • Minimum Percentage:

    Many universities and colleges set a minimum percentage or grade requirement in the 12th-grade board exams, especially in the PCM subjects. The minimum percentage required can vary between institutions.

  • Age Limit:

    The candidates must be a minimum of 17 years of age and a maximum of 23 years.
