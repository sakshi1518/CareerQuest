BMLT (Bachelor of Medical Laboratory Technology)

Duration: 3 years | Entrance Exam: Institute-specific

BMLT is an undergraduate program that trains students in laboratory techniques for medical testing and analysis.After completing BMLT, graduates can work as medical laboratory technologists, performing various laboratory tests and analyses to aid in the diagnosis, treatment, and monitoring of diseases.They can work in quality assurance and accreditation organizations, ensuring that medical laboratories meet regulatory standards and maintain quality in their operations. They can pursue teaching positions in medical laboratory technology colleges and universities.

BOT (Bachelor of Occupational Therapy)

Duration: 4.5years | Entrance Exam: Institute-specific

BOT is an undergraduate program that trains students to help individuals overcome physical and mental challenges in daily activities. Occupational therapists play a crucial role in providing rehabilitation services to individuals who have experienced injuries, strokes, or other medical conditions that affect their ability to perform daily activities. They work to improve functional abilities, enhance mobility, and promote independence.Occupational therapists may find opportunities in various healthcare settings, including hospitals, rehabilitation centers, schools, and private clinics. They may also work with non-governmental organizations (NGOs) and government agencies focusing on disability and rehabilitation services.
