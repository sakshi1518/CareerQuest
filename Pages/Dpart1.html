3-year Bachelor's Degree

Bachelor of Arts

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) degree is an undergraduate postsecondary degree that puts a focus on liberal arts and studies. In comparison, a Bachelor of Science (BS) has a greater focus on science, math, and engineering. The Bachelor of Arts degree is a type of baccalaureate degree.

Bachelor of Science

B.Sc. stands for Bachelor of Science, an undergraduate academic degree typically awarded after three to four years of study in scientific or related fields. Students take courses in disciplines like physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, or computer science, and can pursue various career opportunities upon graduation.

Bachelor of Commerce

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is an undergraduate academic degree program typically completed in three to four years. It focuses on business-related subjects such as accounting, finance, economics, marketing, and management. The degree prepares students for careers in various business fields and provides a solid foundation for higher studies in business and related disciplines.

Other Degrees

Relevant degrees are vital in today's competitive business world. With 1 to 5-year options, students can align their education with career goals, gaining theoretical and practical knowledge for success. Accredited institutions provide quality education, preparing students for leadership roles in dynamic business environments.
