Degree

A three-year degree after 12th is an undergraduate program lasting three years, leading to a Bachelor's degree. It allows students to specialize in a chosen field, gaining foundational knowledge and skills for their career path.

Dual-Degree

A double degree program, also known as a dual degree or combined degree, allows students to pursue two university degrees simultaneously, either at the same institution or different ones, possibly in different countries. These degrees can be in the same subject area or different subjects.

Professinal-Courses

A professional course develops students' knowledge, skills, and training in a specific professional domain. It equips learners with the expertise needed to meet practical requirements, ensuring they are well-prepared for their chosen field.
