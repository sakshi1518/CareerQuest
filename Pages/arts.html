ARTS



  • The Arts stream provides students with a wide variety of academic and career options. Among the many subjects included in the 11th-grade Arts curriculum are Economics, History, Political Science, Geography, Sociology, Philosophy, Psychology, Computer Science, Regional Languages, Hindi, and more. Furthermore, this stream presents more courses for prospective professionals looking to specialize in law, literary studies, journalism and hotel management. With dedicated guidance and dedication to their chosen subject area, students can gain unique knowledge and skills that will influence them through their future endeavors.
