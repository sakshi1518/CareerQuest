Paramedical Courses

  • Diploma in ECG Technology

    Duration: 6 Months to 1 year | Entrance Exam: Institute-specific

    Diploma in ECG Technology is a short-term paramedical course that trains students to perform Electrocardiography (ECG) tests and assist cardiologists in diagnosing heart conditions. ECG technicians assist cardiologists and physicians in performing and interpreting electrocardiograms. They play a vital role in the early detection of heart abnormalities and contribute to the diagnosis and treatment of various cardiac conditions.The Indian government has been investing in strengthening healthcare infrastructure, which includes cardiac care facilities. This may create more job opportunities for ECG technicians in both public and private healthcare sectors.

  • (DRT) Diploma in Radiology Technology

    Duration: 2 to 3 yeara | Entrance Exam: Institute-specific

    DRT is a paramedical course that focuses on medical imaging techniques like X-rays, CT scans, and MRI. Graduates of Diploma in Radiography Technology (DRT) can find employment in hospitals, diagnostic centers, medical imaging centers, and other healthcare facilities. They may also work in specialized departments such as radiology, oncology, orthopedics, and cardiology. The Indian government has been focusing on improving healthcare infrastructure, including the establishment of more medical imaging centers and diagnostic facilities. This may lead to increased job opportunities for DRT professionals in both public and private sectors.

  • Diploma in Operation Theatre Technology (DOTT)

    Duration: 2 years | Entrance Exam: Institute-specific

    DOTT is a paramedical course that trains students to assist in surgical procedures and manage operation theatre equipment. DOTT professionals are responsible for preparing the operation theatre, sterilizing instruments, assisting in patient positioning, handling surgical equipment, and ensuring the safety and hygiene of the surgical environment. The Indian government's focus on improving healthcare infrastructure, including the expansion of surgical facilities, may lead to increased job opportunities for DOTT professionals in both public and private sectors.With experience and further education, DOTT professionals may progress to higher positions in surgical technology, such as senior surgical technologist, operation theatre manager, or educator.

  • Diploma in Dialysis

    Duration: 1 year to 2 year | Entrance Exam: Institute-specific

    Diploma in Dialysis is a paramedical course that trains students to operate dialysis machines and assist patients undergoing kidney dialysis treatments.
